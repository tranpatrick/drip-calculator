DRIP FAUCET CALCULATOR
Drip calculator

This tool computes the cumulative value of your wallet in DRIP and USD over a year using the faucet, depending on the DRIP token price, the initial deposit and the hydrate period in days. It also gives you the details of the taxes payed and interests earned.

HOW TO USE
- Leave the Drip price field empty for auto price if you don't want to simulate this value.
- 2nd field is the number of Drip token you have in your deposit.
- 3rd field is the frequency of compounding in days (1 for everyday, 2 for every 2 days ...).
